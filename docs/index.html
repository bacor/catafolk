Catafolk

A catalogue of folk music datasets for computational ethnomusicology

The project hopes to make datasets of folk music more easily accessible. It provides a reference of datasets, but also aims to include some basic automatic analyses.

Densmore's Teton Sioux Music

A digital edition of 245 songs from the book Teton Sioux Music by Frances Densmore, originally published by the Bureau of American Ethnology, Bulletin 61, Smithsonian Institution (1918). The digital edition was prepared by Craig Stuart Sapp in 2002.

View densmore-teton-sioux
Songs of Ireland

A collection of 62 Irish folk songs from books by Seán O Boyle and Donal O’Sullivan, digitized by Damien Sagrillo.

Pinck's Songs of Lorraine

A collection of 326 folk songs from the Lorraine, first published by Louis Pick between 1926 and 1939, and later digitized by Damien Sagrillo.

Songs of Luxembourg

A large collection of 612 folk songs from Luxembourg. The songs were digitized by Damien Sagrillo from various sources.
